Our Foundation

Rooted in
Community Care

Prismatic Integration is a queer- and trans-led practice built on a grassroots model of care. We offer trauma-informed, nervous system–focused support through massage, breathwork, and hypnotherapy, centering those who have historically been underserved by conventional wellness spaces. Free and low-cost sessions for trans and BIPOC clients are a central, non-negotiable part of how we work,lea not a footnote, but the foundation. These spots are limited; reach out to us directly to connect.

What We Offer

Services

Every service is choice-based and trauma-informed, paced by you, adapted to you, with no expectations about how healing should look or feel.

Therapeutic Massage

In-Person

Customized integrative bodywork drawing from Swedish massage, deep tissue, Thai table massage, myofascial release, craniosacral work, polarity therapy, and energy-based modalities. Each session is shaped around your body and your needs.

Breathwork

Individual & Small Group

Trauma-aware, choice-based breathwork offered one-on-one and in small group settings. Focused on nervous system regulation, embodiment, and emotional processing. Always at a pace that feels right for you.

Hypnotherapy

With Lex  ·  Free & Low-Cost Options Available

Ethical, client-centered hypnotherapy supporting healing, habit change, and self-trust. Free and low-cost support is available for trans and BIPOC clients — spots are limited, so reach out to us directly to learn more.

Online Sessions

Distance  ·  Breathwork & Energy Work

Distance sessions for breathwork and energy-based work, making support accessible regardless of location or mobility.

Making Care Possible

Accessibility & Funding

We believe access to somatic care shouldn't depend on income. Sliding-scale pricing is available across services, and ongoing free and low-cost sessions are held specifically for trans and BIPOC clients. When you pay what you're genuinely able to, you help sustain this model, making it possible for others to receive care who might not otherwise have access.
